About Drake

Welcome to “Drake's World”, the must-visit site for fans of the famous artist Drake! Immerse yourself in the unique universe of this legendary artist, who has left his mark on the world music scene with his hits and unmistakable style. On this site, you'll discover everything that makes Drake so magical

Drake, real name Aubrey Drake Graham, is a multi-faceted artist who has redefined music and culture over the past two decades. Born in Toronto, Canada, he first made a name for himself as an actor in the TV series Degrassi: The Next Generation, before conquering the music world with his debut project “So Far Gone” in 2009.

Today, Drake is one of the most influential artists on the global music scene. With iconic albums such as “Take Care”, “Views”, and “Scorpion”, he has accumulated sales records, prestigious awards, and an international fan base. His unique style blends hip-hop, R&B and pop, while exploring new sounds that push the boundaries of musical genres.
